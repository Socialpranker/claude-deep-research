# Consulting & industry reports

Big consulting firms (McKinsey/BCG/Bain etc), analyst houses (Gartner/Forrester/IDC), и trade associations.

## Big 4 + MBB Consulting (open insights)

### McKinsey & Company

**URL:** https://www.mckinsey.com/insights
**Type:** Consulting
**Access:** OPEN (insights & articles), PAYWALL (specific reports)

**What's inside:**
- Industry insights articles by sector
- Annual reports: State of AI, Tech Trends, Global Banking Report
- McKinsey Global Institute (MGI) reports (more substantial research)

**When to use:**
- Industry overview at executive level
- Market sizing aggregated by sector
- Trend analysis macro level

**How to use:**
- Industries page: `mckinsey.com/industries`
- Insights: `mckinsey.com/our-insights`
- Search pattern: `site:mckinsey.com <topic>`

**Data quality:**
- Credibility: B (industry-respected but with bias)
- Bias: 3 (selling consulting services — projections often boost potential)
- Freshness: regular updates

**Limitations:**
- "Trillion-dollar opportunity" rhetoric — discount headlines
- Methodology often opaque
- Survey samples self-selected

**Combine with:**
- Independent academic research (cross-check claims)
- Multiple consulting firms (triangulate disagreements)

### BCG, Bain, Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG, Accenture

Similar structure to McKinsey. All have:
- `<firm>.com/insights` or `/publications`
- Annual industry reports

**Specific search patterns:**
- BCG: `site:bcg.com/publications <topic>` — `bcg.com/industries` — OPEN
- Bain: `site:bain.com/insights <topic>` — OPEN
- Deloitte: `deloitte.com/insights` — OPEN
- PwC: `pwc.com/gx/en/research-insights` — OPEN
- EY: `ey.com/en_gl/insights` — OPEN
- KPMG: `kpmg.com/xx/en/home/insights.html` — OPEN
- Accenture: `accenture.com/us-en/insights` — OPEN
- Capgemini: `capgemini.com/insights` — OPEN

**Approach:** ищи multiple consulting firms на одну тему, отмечай consensus vs disagreement.

## Analyst houses

### Gartner

**URL:** https://www.gartner.com/en/research
**Type:** Analyst
**Access:** PAYWALL (full reports), OPEN (press releases с key figures)

**What's inside:**
- Magic Quadrant (vendor evaluations)
- Hype Cycle
- Market forecasts
- Industry research

**When to use:**
- Vendor evaluation в enterprise tech
- Tech adoption maturity (Hype Cycle)

**How to use:**
- Press releases: `gartner.com/en/newsroom`
- Search pattern: `<topic> "Magic Quadrant" filetype:pdf`
- Search pattern: `<topic> "Gartner" forecast`

**Data quality:**
- Credibility: B
- Bias: 3-4 (paid vendors influence positioning)
- Historical accuracy на forecasts mixed

**Limitations:**
- Magic Quadrant has vendor selection bias
- Full reports paywalled — work with press releases

**Combine with:**
- Forrester (alternative)
- IDC (alternative)
- Independent academic comparisons

### IDC

**Access:** PARTIAL (press releases with key numbers)

**What's inside:**
- Market share data (server, storage, devices)

**How to use:**

---

### Statista

**URL:** https://www.statista.com/
**Type:** Aggregator
**Access:** PARTIAL (preview free, full data paywall)

**What's inside:**
- 1M+ statistics aggregated from various sources
- Forecasts, market sizes
- Useful as starting point — найти и потом track к original source

**How to use:**
- Search by topic
- Look at preview, then **find original source** they cite
- Search pattern: `site:statista.com <topic>`

**Data quality:**
- Credibility: B (aggregator — quality depends on sources)
- **Always trace к primary source** for citation

**Limitations:**
- Aggregator — secondary source
- Some sources outdated
- Full data behind paywall

## Trade associations (cross-industry overview)

Detail для конкретной industry — в `industries/<industry>.md`. Здесь overview подхода.

**Pattern:** Каждая industry имеет ≥1 ассоциацию. Pattern для поиска:
- `"<industry> association" stats`
- `"<industry> federation" report`
- `<industry> council research`

**Examples (briefs, full в industries/):**
- **Software:** BSA, SIIA
- **Auto:** OICA (global), ACEA (EU), NADA (US)
- **Aviation:** IATA (`iata.org/en/iata-repository/publications`)
- **Tourism:** WTTC, UNWTO
- **Construction:** AGC (US), NAHB (US homebuilders)
- **Restaurants:** National Restaurant Association
- **Hotels:** AHLA, STR (`str.com`)
- **Retail:** NRF (US), BRC (UK), HDE (Germany)
- **Insurance:** III (US), EIOPA (EU)
- **Pharma:** PhRMA (US), EFPIA (EU), IFPMA (global)
- **Manufacturing:** NAM (US), UNIDO (UN)
- **Mining:** ICMM
- **Steel:** worldsteel.org
- **Chemicals:** ACC (US), Cefic (EU)
- **Apparel:** AAFA (US)
- **Software/SaaS:** SaaStock (events with data), no major dedicated assoc

**Quality:** Variable. Industry-funded → bias toward favorable framing. But data often factually accurate (just selective).

## Critical mindset для consulting/analyst data

1. **Always discount headline numbers** by 20-30% (sell-side optimism).
2. **Triangulate** — найди ≥2 firms с close numbers перед citing as fact.
3. **Look for methodology** — если не указано, treat as guideline only.
4. **Check incentive** — кто заплатил за исследование?
5. **Cite multiple firms** — different firms disagree, this is information.

## Combining patterns

**Market sizing claim:**
McKinsey/BCG/Bain (executive narrative) + IDC/Gartner (specific market shares) + Statista (aggregate) + government sources (если есть) + trade association → triangulate.

**Vendor selection enterprise:**
Gartner Magic Quadrant + Forrester Wave + G2/Capterra reviews + customer references + product trial.

**Industry adoption tech:**
Stack Overflow + JetBrains + GitHub Octoverse + State of X report + analyst forecasts.
