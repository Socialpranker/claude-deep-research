## Critical reminders

- **Rankings methodologies differ greatly.** QS weights reputation surveys heavily, ARWU only research. Don't conflate.
- **PISA criticisms** — teaches-to-test, narrow measure. Useful but not the whole picture.
- **Self-reported student outcomes** — College Scorecard earnings data is real (IRS-linked), unlike many other "outcomes" surveys.

## Combining patterns

**Country education quality:** PISA + UNESCO + World Bank EdStats + OECD Education at a Glance + national stat agencies

**US college investment decision:** College Scorecard (earnings/debt) + IPEDS (school details) + Common Data Set (admissions) + US News rankings

**University research strength:** ARWU + Nature Index + citation databases (см. science.md) + faculty CVs
