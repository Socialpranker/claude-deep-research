# Media & entertainment

Film, TV, music, gaming stats.

## Film & TV

### Box Office Mojo
**URL:** boxofficemojo.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Film box office data (US + global, historical), opening weekends, releases tracker
**When:** film performance, box office claims, opening weekend benchmarks
**Quality:** A — IMDb-owned, industry standard
**Combine with:** The Numbers (alternative)

### The Numbers
**URL:** the-numbers.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Film budgets + revenue, financial detail more than Mojo
**When:** budget claims, ROI analysis
**Quality:** A; complementary to Mojo

### IMDb
**URL:** imdb.com/title/<id>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Film/TV metadata, ratings (7M+ titles), cast, crew
**When:** ratings claims, metadata lookup, cast research
**Quality:** B (community ratings — selection bias)

### Rotten Tomatoes
**URL:** rottentomatoes.com/m/<title>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Critic + audience scores aggregated
**When:** critic reception, audience reception
**Quality:** B (aggregator, weighted opinions)

### Metacritic
**URL:** metacritic.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Weighted average reviews for film/TV/games (more curated than RT)
**When:** quality assessment with critic weights

### Letterboxd
**URL:** letterboxd.com/film/<title>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Film social ratings + reviews from cinephile community
**When:** film community sentiment, niche film discovery

### FlixPatrol
**URL:** flixpatrol.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Streaming top 10 by country (Netflix, Disney+, Prime, HBO Max, Apple TV+)
**When:** streaming popularity, по странам
**Quality:** B (derived from platform top 10 lists)

### Parrot Analytics
**URL:** parrotanalytics.com/insights
**Access:** PARTIAL
**What:** TV demand metrics (social, search, etc)
**When:** TV demand analytics

### Nielsen
**URL:** nielsen.com — press releases only
**Access:** LIMITED
**What:** TV ratings (US), media measurement
**Quality:** A but mostly paywall

## Music

### Spotify Charts
**URL:** charts.spotify.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Spotify top tracks/artists/albums by region, daily
**When:** music popularity by region, streaming hits

### MusicBrainz
**URL:** musicbrainz.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Open music metadata database (artists, releases, recordings)
**When:** music data lookup, discography research
**Quality:** B (community-maintained)

### Last.fm
**URL:** last.fm/music/<artist>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Scrobble data (user listens), tags, similar artists
**When:** music listening patterns, similar artist discovery

### RIAA Gold/Platinum
**URL:** riaa.com/gold-platinum
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US music certifications database
**When:** US sales certifications

### BPI
**URL:** bpi.co.uk
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** UK music certifications
**When:** UK sales certifications

### Chartmetric
**URL:** chartmetric.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (basic profiles free)
**What:** Music analytics across platforms — streams, social, playlists
**When:** music industry analytics

### Setlist.fm
**URL:** setlist.fm/setlists/<artist>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Concert setlist database (community)
**When:** tour data, setlist research

### YouTube Music (через Social Blade)
**URL:** socialblade.com/youtube
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** YouTube channel stats for music

## Gaming

### Steam DB
**URL:** steamdb.info/app/<id>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Steam game stats — concurrent players, price history, owners estimate
**When:** Steam game performance, price history
**Quality:** A (Steam API derived)

### Steam Charts
**URL:** steamcharts.com/app/<id>
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Historical concurrent player counts
**When:** Steam game player trend over time

### ActivePlayer.io
**URL:** activeplayer.io
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Live player counts across many games (multi-platform)
**When:** game popularity comparison

### Newzoo
**URL:** newzoo.com/insights
**Access:** PARTIAL (insights free, full reports paywall)
**What:** Gaming market intelligence — global market size, esports
**When:** gaming market sizing claims
**Quality:** B (estimates)

### Esports Charts
**URL:** escharts.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Esports tournament viewership data
**When:** esports specific analytics

### Twitch Tracker
**URL:** twitchtracker.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Twitch streamer + game stats (already in consumer_digital)
**When:** game popularity на Twitch

### Roblox / GitHyp
**URL:** rolimon's.com (Roblox), githyp.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Roblox economy, game popularity
**When:** specific to Roblox / game popularity

### OpenCritic
**URL:** opencritic.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Game review aggregator (alternative к Metacritic)
**When:** game critic reception

## Quick reference

|---|---|

| Film/TV rating | IMDb + Rotten Tomatoes + Metacritic |
| Streaming top 10 by country | FlixPatrol |

## Combining patterns

**Film success analysis:** Box Office Mojo + The Numbers + IMDb + Metacritic + RT + Letterboxd (cinephile reception)

**Music artist analysis:** Spotify Charts + Last.fm + Chartmetric + RIAA/BPI certifications + YouTube Social Blade + setlist.fm для touring

**Steam game launch analysis:** Steam DB + Steam Charts + OpenCritic + Twitch (streaming reception) + Reddit community sentiment

**Streaming TV demand:** Parrot Analytics + FlixPatrol + IMDb ratings + audience scores + show subreddit activity
