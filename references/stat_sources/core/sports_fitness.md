# Sports & fitness

Sports stats, esports, fitness aggregates.

## Major US sports

### ESPN Stats
**URL:** espn.com/<sport>/stats
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, NCAA stats
**When:** US sport stats
**Quality:** A

### Pro Football Reference / Basketball Reference / Baseball Reference / Hockey Reference
**URL:** pro-football-reference.com, basketball-reference.com, baseball-reference.com, hockey-reference.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Comprehensive sports databases — historical stats, player game logs, advanced metrics
**When:** deep historical sports research, advanced stats
**Quality:** A — gold standard for US sport history

### Sports Reference (overall)
**URL:** sports-reference.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Umbrella for the above + soccer, college
**When:** sports research starting point

### Stathead
**URL:** stathead.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (paid for advanced queries)
**What:** Sports Reference's advanced query tool
**When:** specific stat combinations

## Soccer / Football (global)

### Transfermarkt
**URL:** transfermarkt.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Player market values, transfers, club rosters globally
**When:** football transfer market analysis, player valuations
**Quality:** B (community-derived market values)

### FBref (football reference)
**URL:** fbref.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Advanced football stats — xG, passing networks
**When:** football analytics
**Quality:** A

### Understat
**URL:** understat.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** xG-focused football stats top leagues
**When:** football xG analysis

### Whoscored
**URL:** whoscored.com

**What:** Football statistics ratings
**When:** match analytics

### Opta / StatsPerform
**URL:** statsperform.com

**What:** Pro football data
**When:** through derived sources

## Combining patterns

**Player evaluation (football):**
Transfermarkt (market value) + FBref (advanced stats) + Understat (xG) + Opta-derived sources + club's own data

**Esports tournament analysis:**
Esports Charts (viewership) + Liquipedia (results) + Esports Earnings (prize) + Twitch Tracker (streamer engagement)

**Olympic medal trends:**
Olympedia (historical) + IOC official + national olympic committees
