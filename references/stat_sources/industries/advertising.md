# Digital Advertising / Marketing

## Tier 1

### IAB (Interactive Advertising Bureau)
**URL:** iab.com/insights
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Digital ad industry data — annual IAB/PwC Internet Ad Revenue Report
**When:** digital ad spend market sizing US
**Quality:** A (industry body, audited)

### eMarketer / Insider Intelligence
**URL:** insiderintelligence.com
**Access:** PARTIAL
**What:** Digital ad forecasts global
**When:** global digital ad market projections

### MAGNA Global
**URL:** magnaglobal.com/insights
**Access:** OPEN reports
**What:** Ad spending forecasts global
**When:** alternative to eMarketer

### WARC
**URL:** warc.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (annual advertising expenditure)
**What:** Global advertising data
**When:** global ad spending alternative

## Platform-specific

### Google Ads / Meta Ads
- Their **earnings calls** have substantial ad revenue commentary
- **Google's economic impact reports** — `economicimpact.google.com` — OPEN
- **Meta's transparency reports** — `transparency.meta.com`

### Facebook/Meta Ad Library
**URL:** facebook.com/ads/library
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** All active Facebook/Instagram ads searchable
**When:** competitor ad analysis

### Google Ads Transparency Center
**URL:** adstransparency.google.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Google ads from advertisers
**When:** competitor ad analysis (Google side)

### TikTok Ad Library
**URL:** library.tiktok.com
**Access:** OPEN

### Twitter/X Ads
Limited transparency since 2023 changes.

## Combining patterns

**Global ad market sizing:**
IAB (US digital) + eMarketer + MAGNA + WARC (cross-check) + Group M reports + analyst houses

**Competitor ad analysis:**
Meta Ad Library + Google Ads Transparency + TikTok Ad Library + their public marketing channels

**Channel performance benchmarks:**
WordStream + Tinuiti + AdEspresso + Statista benchmarks
