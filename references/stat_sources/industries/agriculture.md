# Agriculture & Food

## Tier 1

### FAOSTAT
**URL:** fao.org/faostat
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** UN FAO — global agricultural production, prices, food security, fertilizers, livestock, trade
**When:** any global agri/food claim
**Quality:** A
**Limitations:** 1-2 year lag, country self-reports

### USDA
**URL:** usda.gov/topics/data
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US agriculture detailed — production, prices, trade, market reports
**When:** US agriculture
**Key portals:**
- **NASS** (`nass.usda.gov`) — National Agricultural Statistics Service
- **ERS** (`ers.usda.gov`) — Economic Research Service
- **FAS** (`fas.usda.gov`) — Foreign Agricultural Service

### Eurostat Agriculture
**URL:** ec.europa.eu/eurostat/web/agriculture
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU agriculture data — production, prices, structure
**When:** EU agriculture

## Tier 2

### OECD-FAO Agricultural Outlook
**URL:** oecd-ilibrary.org/agriculture-and-food
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** 10-year outlook for major commodities (annual)
**When:** medium-term agricultural projections

### USDA WASDE (World Agricultural Supply and Demand Estimates)
**URL:** usda.gov/oce/commodity-markets/wasde
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Monthly global crop supply/demand estimates
**When:** commodity market analysis

### CGIAR
**URL:** cgiar.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global agricultural research consortium
**When:** agricultural research

### IGC (International Grains Council)
**URL:** igc.int
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global grains trade data
**When:** grains specifically

### Sugar / Coffee / Cocoa
- **ISO (Int Sugar Org)** — `isosugar.org` — partial
- **ICO (Int Coffee Org)** — `ico.org` — OPEN
- **ICCO (Int Cocoa Org)** — `icco.org` — partial

### Livestock specific
- **FAO Livestock** — `fao.org/livestock-systems`
- **USDA Animal & Plant Health** — `aphis.usda.gov`
- **OIE (World Org for Animal Health)** — `woah.org`

### Food prices
- **FAO Food Price Index** — monthly
- **USDA Food Prices** — `ers.usda.gov/data-products/food-price-outlook`

### Sustainability / Food security
- **GHI (Global Hunger Index)** — `globalhungerindex.org` — OPEN annual
- **WFP (UN World Food Programme)** — `wfp.org/data`
- **IFPRI** — `ifpri.org` — research

## Combining patterns

**Country agricultural overview:**
FAOSTAT (global comparable) + national stat agency (USDA, EU MS agencies) + WTO Trade (exports) + OECD-FAO Outlook (projections)

**Global commodity prices:**
USDA WASDE + FAO Food Price Index + commodity exchanges (CME, ICE) + IGC (grains)

**Food security:**
WFP data + FAO + GHI + IFPRI research + country-level surveys
