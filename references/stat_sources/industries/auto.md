# Auto / Mobility / EV

## Tier 1

### OICA
**URL:** oica.net
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** International Org of Motor Vehicle Manufacturers — global production + sales by country
**When:** global auto production/sales claims
**Quality:** A

### ACEA
**URL:** acea.auto
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** European Automobile Manufacturers Association — EU registrations monthly
**When:** EU auto market

### InsideEVs
**URL:** insideevs.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EV news + monthly sales data per country/model
**When:** EV adoption tracking
**Quality:** B (news source aggregating data)
**Key:** monthly sales scorecards

## Combining patterns

**Global EV adoption:** InsideEVs + EV-Volumes + IEA Global EV Outlook + OICA total auto context

**Country auto market:** national association (e.g., ACEA EU, JADA Japan, ANFAVEA Brazil) + OICA + manufacturer announcements

**EV vs ICE economics:** Lazard LCOE (electricity) + BloombergNEF EV outlook + IEA + national fuel prices
