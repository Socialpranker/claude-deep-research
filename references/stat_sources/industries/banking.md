# Banking & Financial services

## Tier 1

### FDIC
**URL:** fdic.gov/analysis
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US banks data — Quarterly Banking Profile, Call Reports for every US bank
**When:** US bank-specific data, US banking system health
**Quality:** A
**Key:** FDIC's BankFind за individual bank financials

### ECB Statistical Data Warehouse
**URL:** sdw.ecb.europa.eu
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU banking statistics, supervisory data
**When:** EU banking research
**См.** core/gov_macro.md

### BIS (Bank for International Settlements)
**URL:** bis.org/statistics
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Cross-border banking flows, currency markets, financial stability
**When:** international banking, currency
**См.** core/gov_macro.md

### Federal Reserve H.8
**URL:** federalreserve.gov/releases/h8
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Weekly US commercial bank assets/liabilities
**When:** real-time US banking conditions

## Tier 2

### Federal Reserve Senior Loan Officer Survey (SLOOS)
**URL:** federalreserve.gov/data/sloos
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Quarterly US bank lending standards survey
**When:** US credit conditions analysis

### EBA (European Banking Authority)
**URL:** eba.europa.eu/risk-analysis-and-data
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU bank-specific data, stress tests
**When:** EU bank-specific research

### S&P Global Market Intelligence Banking
**URL:** spglobal.com/marketintelligence
**Access:** PARTIAL
**What:** Bank financial data + analytics
**When:** comparable bank metrics

### Federal Reserve Z.1 Flow of Funds
**URL:** federalreserve.gov/releases/z1
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US financial accounts — household + business + government balance sheets
**When:** macro financial flows

## Specific areas

### Payments
- **Visa, Mastercard** — annual reports + IR pages with payment volume data
- **BIS Statistics on Payment Systems** (CPMI)
- **EU Payment Statistics** (ECB)
- **Federal Reserve Payments Study** — every 3 years US payments research

### Fintech
- **CB Insights State of Fintech** — partial OPEN
- **FT Partners** — fintech reports — OPEN
- **PitchBook Fintech** — paywall, press
- **a16z Fintech** — `a16z.com/fintech` — OPEN essays

### Mortgage data US
- **Mortgage Bankers Association (MBA)** — `mba.org/news-and-research` — OPEN partial
- **Freddie Mac Primary Mortgage Market Survey** — OPEN weekly rates
- **Federal Reserve Mortgage Data** — OPEN

### Credit card / consumer debt
- **Federal Reserve G.19** — consumer credit — OPEN
- **NY Fed Household Debt Report** — OPEN quarterly
- **CFPB Consumer Credit Data** — OPEN

## Combining patterns

**Bank-specific due diligence (US):**
FDIC BankFind (Call Reports) + bank's 10-K (если public — companies_public.md) + Federal Reserve H.8 (system context) + analyst coverage

**US banking system health:**
FDIC Quarterly Banking Profile + Fed H.8 + Fed SLOOS (lending standards) + NY Fed Household Debt

**EU banking comparative:**
EBA bank-by-bank + ECB SDW (aggregates) + national supervisors

**Fintech landscape:**
CB Insights State of Fintech + FT Partners reports + a16z + PitchBook press + specific company sources (companies_private.md)

**Macro credit cycle:**
Fed Z.1 Flow of Funds + BIS + national central banks + commercial bank data
