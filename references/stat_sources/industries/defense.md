# Defense & Aerospace

## Tier 1

### SIPRI (Stockholm International Peace Research Institute)
**URL:** sipri.org/databases
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Military expenditure database, arms transfers, peace operations
**When:** military spending claims, arms trade
**Quality:** A — independent, well-respected

### Jane's (Janes)
**URL:** janes.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (mostly paywall)
**What:** Defense intelligence (Jane's Fighting Ships, Jane's All the World's Aircraft)
**When:** specific military hardware research (через press releases)
**Quality:** A

### IISS Military Balance
**URL:** iiss.org/publications/the-military-balance
**Access:** PARTIAL (summaries free)
**What:** Annual assessment of military capabilities of states
**When:** country military capability comparison

## Tier 2

### CSIS (Center for Strategic and International Studies)
**URL:** csis.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Defense + security research
**When:** geopolitical / defense analysis

### RAND Corporation
**URL:** rand.org/topics/national-security-and-terrorism.html
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Defense + national security research
**When:** policy-grade defense research

### Defense News Top 100
**URL:** defensenews.com/top-100
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Annual ranking of defense companies by revenue
**When:** defense industry market shares

### NATO STATs
**URL:** nato.int/cps/en/natohq/topics_49198.htm
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** NATO defense expenditure data
**When:** NATO context

### US Defense Budget / Programs
- **Department of Defense** — `defense.gov`
- **SAM.gov** — `sam.gov` — federal contracts
- **USAspending.gov** — defense contracts subset

### Specific
- **DefenseOne** + **Breaking Defense** + **Defense News** — news with embedded data — OPEN
- **AEI Critical Threats Project** — `criticalthreats.org` — partial
- **Center for a New American Security** (CNAS) — `cnas.org`

## Conflict data

### ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data)
**URL:** acleddata.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Real-time conflict event data
**When:** conflict research

### UCDP (Uppsala Conflict Data Program)
**URL:** ucdp.uu.se
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Armed conflict + violence database
**When:** academic conflict research

## Combining patterns

**Country military capability:**
SIPRI (spending) + IISS Military Balance (force structure) + Janes (specific systems) + national defense MoD + CSIS analysis

**Defense industry market:**
Defense News Top 100 + company 10-Ks (US public defense contractors — Lockheed, RTX, BA, etc) + SIPRI + NATO data + USAspending (US contracts)

**Conflict analysis:**
ACLED (real-time) + UCDP (historical) + news context + ICRC humanitarian + CSIS/RAND analysis
