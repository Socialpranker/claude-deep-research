# Energy / Oil & Gas

## Tier 1

### EIA (US Energy Information Administration)
**URL:** eia.gov
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US (+ global subset) energy data — production, consumption, prices, fuel mix, electricity, oil, gas, renewables
**When:** US energy claims, oil/gas markets, electricity sector
**Quality:** A (US gov)
**How:** Browse by topic; `eia.gov/totalenergy` для time series; API free
**Key reports:** STEO (Short-Term Energy Outlook), AEO (Annual Energy Outlook), International Energy Outlook
**Limitations:** US-focused; international data subset only
**Combine with:** IEA для global

### IEA (International Energy Agency)
**URL:** iea.org/data-and-statistics
**Access:** PARTIAL OPEN (many free reports + paid data)
**What:** Global energy stats — supply, demand, carbon intensity, electricity, oil
**When:** global energy transition research
**Quality:** A
**Key reports:** World Energy Outlook, Renewables, Electricity Market Report, Oil Market Report

### BP Statistical Review (Energy Institute)
**URL:** energyinstitute.org/statistical-review
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Annual statistical review of world energy — historical fuel mix per country (60+ years)
**When:** long-term energy time series, country fuel mix history
**Quality:** A

## Tier 2

### OPEC
**URL:** opec.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Oil prices, production, OPEC member data, monthly oil market report
**When:** oil-specific analysis

### EIA Petroleum
**URL:** eia.gov/petroleum
**Specific:** weekly US petroleum data — inventories, refinery utilization

### S&P Global Platts
**URL:** spglobal.com/commodityinsights
**Access:** PARTIAL
**What:** Energy benchmarks, prices
**When:** specific commodity price data

### Rystad Energy
**URL:** rystadenergy.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (press releases)
**What:** Upstream oil & gas intelligence
**When:** through press releases

### Wood Mackenzie
**URL:** woodmac.com
**Access:** PAYWALL with press releases
**What:** Energy market analysis
**When:** через published reports/press

### Ember (electricity, climate)
**URL:** ember-energy.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global electricity transition data — coal/gas/renewables
**When:** electricity decarbonization research
**Quality:** A — independent think tank

### IRENA (renewables)
**URL:** irena.org/Data
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Renewable energy data global
**When:** renewables-specific

### Lazard LCOE
**URL:** lazard.com/research-insights/levelized-cost-of-energy
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Levelized cost of energy by technology (annual)
**When:** energy technology cost comparison

## Combining patterns

**Country energy transition:** EIA (US) or national stat + IEA (global comparable) + BP/Energy Institute (historical) + Ember (electricity specific) + IRENA (renewables)

**Oil market analysis:** EIA Weekly Petroleum + OPEC Monthly + IEA Oil Market Report + price benchmarks (Brent/WTI through Platts)

**Renewable economics:** IRENA + Lazard LCOE + IEA Renewables + EIA renewable data
