# ESG / Sustainability metrics

## Tier 1

### CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project)
**URL:** cdp.net/en/data
**Access:** OPEN (public scores)
**What:** Company climate disclosures — emissions, climate strategy, water, forests
**When:** corporate climate disclosure validation
**Quality:** A
**Key:** A/A-/B/C/D scoring per company

### SBTi (Science Based Targets initiative)
**URL:** sciencebasedtargets.org/companies-taking-action
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Validated science-based climate targets by company
**When:** company climate target validation

### Climate TRACE
**URL:** climatetrace.org
**Access:** OPEN
**См.** core/climate_env.md

### MSCI ESG Research
**URL:** msci.com/our-solutions/esg-investing
**Access:** PARTIAL (overview free)
**What:** ESG ratings + index data
**When:** institutional ESG benchmarks

## Tier 2

### Sustainalytics
**URL:** sustainalytics.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (company snapshots free)
**What:** ESG risk ratings
**When:** alternative ESG ratings

### GRI Standards
**URL:** globalreporting.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Sustainability reporting framework standards
**When:** ESG reporting framework reference

### SASB Standards
**URL:** sasb.org
**Access:** OPEN (now part of IFRS)
**What:** Industry-specific sustainability accounting standards
**When:** materiality framework

### TCFD
**URL:** fsb-tcfd.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures
**When:** climate disclosure framework

### Bloomberg ESG Data
Behind paywall, but corporate sustainability reports + 10-K ESG sections OPEN.

### Sustainability Reports
**URL:** responsibilityreports.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Aggregator of company sustainability reports
**When:** find specific company report

### Refinitiv ESG Database
Paywall, but some indices free.

### EU Taxonomy
**URL:** finance.ec.europa.eu/sustainable-finance/tools-and-standards/eu-taxonomy-sustainable-activities_en
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU taxonomy of sustainable activities
**When:** EU sustainability compliance

### ISSB (International Sustainability Standards Board)
**URL:** ifrs.org/sustainability
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global sustainability disclosure standards (newer)
**When:** future disclosure standards

## Climate / Carbon

### Greenhouse Gas Protocol
**URL:** ghgprotocol.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Scope 1/2/3 emissions accounting standard
**When:** emissions methodology reference

### Project Drawdown
**URL:** drawdown.org/solutions
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Climate solutions ranked by potential
**When:** climate solution prioritization

### Climate Action Tracker
См. core/climate_env.md.

## Combining patterns

**Company ESG due diligence:**
CDP (climate score) + Sustainalytics + MSCI ESG (ratings) + SBTi (targets validated) + company sustainability report (TCFD-aligned ideally) + Climate TRACE (independent emissions verification)

**Industry ESG benchmarks:**
SASB Standards (materiality) + GRI Standards + MSCI ESG industry breakdowns + analyst houses

**Climate solutions / pathways:**
Project Drawdown + IEA Net Zero scenarios + IPCC mitigation pathways + national NDCs (Climate Action Tracker)
