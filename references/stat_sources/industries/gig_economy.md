# Gig Economy / Platform Work

## Tier 1

### JPMorgan Chase Institute
**URL:** jpmorganchase.com/institute
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Gig economy research based on JPM Chase account data
**When:** US gig income patterns research

### Pew Research Gig Work
**URL:** pewresearch.org (search "gig")
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Survey research on platform/gig work
**When:** worker attitudes, prevalence of gig work

### Online Labour Index (Oxford)
**URL:** onlinelabourobservatory.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Oxford research tracking online gig work supply by country
**When:** global online gig market research
**Quality:** A — academic

### Fairwork Foundation
**URL:** fair.work
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Ratings of platform work conditions (Uber, etc) globally
**When:** worker conditions research

## Combining patterns

**US gig economy size:**
JPMorgan Chase Institute + MBO Partners + Pew Research + BLS Contingent Worker Supplement + platform 10-Ks (Uber, DASH, Lyft)

**Worker conditions:**
Fairwork (independent ratings) + ILO + national labor agency surveys + journalism (e.g., NYT/Wired investigation series)

**Global gig market:**
Online Labour Index + ILO + Upwork/Fiverr 10-Ks + Doordash/Uber international filings
