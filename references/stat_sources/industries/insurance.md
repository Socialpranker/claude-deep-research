# Insurance

## Tier 1

### NAIC (US insurance regulators)
**URL:** naic.org/research-statistics
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US insurance industry data — financial filings (Statutory Annual Statements), market share
**When:** US insurance industry research
**Quality:** A

### EIOPA (EU insurance regulator)
**URL:** eiopa.europa.eu/tools-and-data
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU insurance statistics, supervision data
**When:** EU insurance research

### Swiss Re Sigma Reports
**URL:** swissre.com/institute/research/sigma-research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Annual reports on global insurance markets, by line of business, by country
**When:** global insurance overview
**Quality:** A — industry standard reference

### Insurance Information Institute (III)
**URL:** iii.org/facts-statistics
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US insurance facts + statistics
**When:** US insurance industry context (industry-funded but factual)

## Combining patterns

**US insurer due diligence:**
NAIC filings + A.M. Best rating + III industry context + company's own annual report (если public — see companies_public.md) + news coverage

**Global insurance market:**
Swiss Re Sigma + OECD Insurance Statistics + EIOPA (EU) + NAIC (US) + Lloyd's (specialty) + Geneva Association (research)

**Climate / NatCat insurance:**
Munich Re NatCatService + Swiss Re Sigma + Lloyd's of London + climate science context (см. core/climate_env.md)
