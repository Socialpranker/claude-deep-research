# IT Services / Outsourcing

## Tier 1

### NASSCOM
**URL:** nasscom.in/knowledge-center
**Access:** OPEN partial
**What:** Indian IT industry data (largest IT services exporter)
**When:** India IT industry research, global IT services market context
**Quality:** A — industry standard для India

### ISG (Information Services Group)
**URL:** isg-one.com/research
**Access:** OPEN reports
**What:** ISG Index — quarterly tracking of outsourcing/managed services deals
**When:** outsourcing/managed services market tracking
**Quality:** A

### Gartner IT Spending Forecasts
**URL:** gartner.com (через press releases)
**Access:** PARTIAL (press releases с key numbers)
**What:** Annual IT spending forecasts by segment
**When:** IT spending market sizing
**См.** core/consulting_industry.md

## Combining patterns

**IT services market sizing:**
Gartner IT Spending + IDC + NASSCOM (India share) + ISG Index (deal volume) + analyst houses cross-check

**Outsourcing market:**
ISG Index quarterly + Everest Group + HfS Research + Computer Economics benchmarks

**Cloud services shares:**
Synergy Research + Canalys + Gartner press + AWS/Azure/Google earnings calls
