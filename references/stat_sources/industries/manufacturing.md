# Manufacturing

## Tier 1

### ISM Manufacturing PMI (US)
**URL:** ismworld.org
**Access:** OPEN (headline numbers)
**What:** US Manufacturing PMI — leading indicator (>50 expansion, <50 contraction)
**When:** US manufacturing cycle
**Quality:** A

### S&P Global Manufacturing PMI (Global)
**URL:** pmi.spglobal.com
**Access:** OPEN headlines
**What:** Manufacturing PMI for 40+ countries
**When:** global manufacturing cycle

### UNIDO Stats
**URL:** stat.unido.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** UN Industrial Development Org — global manufacturing data
**When:** developing country manufacturing

### Eurostat Industrial Production
**URL:** ec.europa.eu/eurostat
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU industrial production index, capacity utilization
**When:** EU manufacturing trends

### Federal Reserve Industrial Production
**URL:** federalreserve.gov/releases/g17
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US industrial production index, capacity utilization
**When:** US manufacturing trends (monthly)

## Tier 2

### NAM (National Association of Manufacturers)
**URL:** nam.org/issues/economy-and-trade
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US manufacturers' lobbying organization — outlook surveys, policy positions
**When:** US manufacturing industry view (with industry bias)

### MAPI Foundation
**URL:** mapifoundation.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US manufacturing R&D research
**When:** manufacturing innovation research

### Census Manufacturing
**URL:** census.gov/manufacturing
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US Annual Survey of Manufactures + Economic Census
**When:** detailed US manufacturing structure (every 5 years deep, annual updates)

### China NBS Industrial
**URL:** stats.gov.cn
**Access:** OPEN partial
**What:** China industrial output, profits
**When:** China manufacturing (with caveats — verify через external)

## Specific sub-industries

- **Steel:** worldsteel.org — OPEN
- **Aluminum:** world-aluminium.org — OPEN
- **Cement:** cembureau.eu (EU), portlandcement.org (US)
- **Chemicals:** ACC (`americanchemistry.com`), Cefic (`cefic.org`) — OPEN
- **Machinery:** VDMA Germany (`vdma.org`) — OPEN
- **Semiconductors:** SIA (`semiconductors.org`), WSTS (`wsts.org`) — partial OPEN

## Combining patterns

**Manufacturing cycle indicator:**
ISM PMI + S&P Global Manufacturing PMI + Fed Industrial Production + Eurostat (cross-region)

**US manufacturing deep:**
Census Manufacturing + Fed Industrial Production + ISM + NAM (industry view) + sector-specific (steel, chemicals, etc.)

**China manufacturing (with skepticism):**
China NBS + S&P Global Chinese PMI (independent) + satellite-based estimates (e.g., night lights) + China-watcher analysts (CSIS, Rhodium Group)
