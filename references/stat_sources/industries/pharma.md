# Pharmaceuticals / Biotech

## Tier 1

### FDA Drugs@FDA
**URL:** accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cder
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US drug approvals, labels, applications
**When:** US drug regulatory status
**Quality:** A

### EMA
**URL:** ema.europa.eu
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU drug approvals, EPAR (assessment reports)
**When:** EU drug regulatory

### ClinicalTrials.gov + EU Clinical Trials
См. core/health.md.

### IQVIA
**URL:** iqvia.com/insights
**Access:** PARTIAL (insights free, full data paywall)
**What:** Pharma market data — sales by drug/region, prescribing patterns
**When:** pharma market sizing, drug sales rankings
**Quality:** A — industry standard

## Tier 2

### EvaluatePharma
**URL:** evaluatepharma.com — paywall, press releases partial
**Access:** PARTIAL
**What:** Pharma forecasts, deals, pipelines
**When:** через press releases

### GlobalData Healthcare
**URL:** globaldata.com/industries-we-cover/pharmaceuticals
**Access:** PARTIAL
**What:** Pharma intelligence
**When:** alternative to IQVIA

### EFPIA (Europe)
**URL:** efpia.eu/about-medicines/use-of-medicines/data-center
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** European pharma industry data
**When:** EU pharma overview (industry self-report)

### PhRMA (US)
**URL:** phrma.org/policy-issues/research-and-development
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US pharma industry data + R&D facts
**When:** US pharma industry context (industry self-report)

### IFPMA (global)
**URL:** ifpma.org
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global research-based pharma industry data

### NIH RePORTER
**URL:** reporter.nih.gov
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** NIH-funded research project database
**When:** US government-funded biomedical R&D

### BIO / SVB / Citeline (biotech investment)
- BIO Industry Analysis — partial reports
- SVB Healthcare Outlook — OPEN annual reports
- Citeline (Informa Pharma) — paywall

### Drug pricing
- **GoodRx** (`goodrx.com`) — US drug prices
- **IFPMA Pricing & Reimbursement** — global pricing context
- **CMS Drug Spending** (`cms.gov`) — US Medicare/Medicaid drug spending

## Pipeline / Pre-clinical research

- **BioCentury** — partial
- **Endpoints News** (`endpts.com`) — OPEN, pharma news
- **STAT News** (`statnews.com`) — OPEN, pharma/biotech news

## Combining patterns

**Drug market analysis:** IQVIA (sales) + Drugs@FDA + EMA (regulatory) + ClinicalTrials.gov (pipeline) + analyst reports (EvaluatePharma press) + Endpoints/STAT news

**Pipeline analysis:** ClinicalTrials.gov + company pipelines (10-K) + GlobalData/EvaluatePharma press + scientific literature (PubMed) + conference proceedings (academic channel)

**Pharma industry economics:** EFPIA/PhRMA/IFPMA (industry self-report) + IQVIA + national health systems data (drug spending) + analyst houses
