# Real Estate (commercial + residential)

## Tier 1 — Commercial

### CBRE Research
**URL:** cbre.com/insights/research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global commercial real estate research, market reports per city/sector
**When:** CRE market analysis
**Quality:** A — broker bias mitigated by depth

### Cushman & Wakefield
**URL:** cushmanwakefield.com/en/insights
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Alternative CRE research
**When:** cross-check CBRE

### JLL Research
**URL:** jll.com/en/trends-and-insights/research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global CRE research
**When:** alternative CRE source

### Knight Frank
**URL:** knightfrank.com/research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global residential + CRE — especially luxury, wealth reports
**When:** luxury residential, prime markets

## Tier 1 — Residential US

### Zillow Research
**URL:** zillow.com/research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US housing market data — prices, inventory, rentals
**When:** US residential trends
**Quality:** B (estimates from Zestimate methodology)

### Redfin Data Center
**URL:** redfin.com/news/data-center
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US home sales, prices, inventory weekly
**When:** US residential real-time

### Case-Shiller (S&P)
**URL:** spglobal.com/spdji/en/index-family/indicators/sp-corelogic-case-shiller
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US home price index — methodologically rigorous
**When:** US home price reference

### NAR (National Association of Realtors)
**URL:** nar.realtor/research-and-statistics
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Existing home sales, pending sales, affordability index
**When:** US residential market data
**Quality:** B (industry self-report — affordability claims with bias)

## Combining patterns

**US residential market:**
Zillow + Redfin (current) + Case-Shiller (rigorous methodology) + NAR (industry) + Census Housing (official) + NAHB (builder sentiment)

**Global CRE:**
CBRE + Cushman & Wakefield + JLL (triangulate three big brokers) + Knight Frank (luxury) + MSCI/NCREIF (institutional returns)

**Hospitality:**
STR (hotels) + CBRE Hotels + AirDNA (STR) + UNWTO context (см. transport_travel.md)

**City housing affordability:**
Numbeo + local statistics + Zillow (US) + Knight Frank Global Residential Report
