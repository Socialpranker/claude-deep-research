# Retail & E-commerce

## Tier 1

### National Retail Federation (NRF)
**URL:** nrf.com/research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** US retail association data — monthly sales, holiday spending, top retailers
**When:** US retail trends, holiday forecasts
**Quality:** B (industry self-report)

### US Census Retail Trade
**URL:** census.gov/retail
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Official US retail sales by category (monthly)
**When:** US retail size validation
**Quality:** A — gov source

### Eurocommerce
**URL:** eurocommerce.eu
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU retail association — data + advocacy
**When:** EU retail context

## Combining patterns

**US retail landscape:**
US Census Retail Trade (official sales) + NRF (industry view) + Digital Commerce 360 (online ranking) + analyst forecasts (eMarketer) + specific retailer SEC filings (если public)

**Amazon marketplace:**
Marketplace Pulse + Junglescout + Amazon press releases + SEC filings (10-K mentions) + Helium 10 third-party

**Holiday season retail:**
NRF forecast + Adobe Digital Insights + Salesforce Holiday Insights + government release after fact (Census)
