# Telecom / Connectivity

## Tier 1

### ITU
**URL:** itu.int/en/ITU-D/Statistics
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** International Telecommunication Union — global telecom indicators (mobile/fixed subs, internet, 5G)
**When:** global telecom statistics
**Quality:** A

### GSMA Intelligence
**URL:** gsmaintelligence.com
**Access:** PARTIAL (some reports free)
**What:** Mobile industry data — subscribers, ARPU, technology adoption
**When:** mobile industry analysis
**Quality:** A (industry standard)

### Ookla / Speedtest
**URL:** speedtest.net/global-index
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Global broadband/mobile speeds by country
**When:** connectivity speed claims

### Cloudflare Radar
**URL:** radar.cloudflare.com
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Internet traffic patterns, popular domains, outages
**When:** macro internet trends
**См.** core/consumer_digital.md

### OFCOM (UK)

**When:** UK telecom research

### BEREC (EU regulators)

**Access:** OPEN
**What:** EU telecom regulator umbrella
**When:** EU telecom policy

### OpenSignal
**URL:** opensignal.com/reports
**Access:** OPEN

### Cable.co.uk Worldwide Broadband Pricing
**URL:** cable.co.uk/broadband/research

**When:** broadband cost comparisons

## Internet / Backbone

### APNIC
**URL:** apnic.net/community/research
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Asia-Pacific network research, IPv6 adoption

### Hurricane Electric BGP Toolkit
**URL:** bgp.he.net
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** BGP routing data — AS-level internet topology
**When:** internet infrastructure research

### M-Lab (Measurement Lab)
**URL:** measurementlab.net/data
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Internet performance measurements global open data
**When:** scientific internet research

### Akamai State of the Internet
**URL:** akamai.com/our-thinking/the-state-of-the-internet
**Access:** OPEN
**What:** Quarterly internet trends — speed, security
**When:** internet macro trends (legacy reports still useful)

## 5G specific

## Satellite / Space

- **SatelliteRegistry** — partial
- **CelesTrak** (`celestrak.org`) — OPEN, satellite tracking
- **Bryce Tech** — `brycetech.com/reports` — OPEN, space industry

## Combining patterns

**Mobile operator comparison:**
GSMA Intelligence + OpenSignal (performance) + national regulator data + operator IR reports

**Internet infrastructure:**
APNIC + Hurricane Electric BGP + Cloudflare Radar + Akamai reports + M-Lab data

**5G deployment status:**
GSA tracker + GSMA Intelligence + national regulators + operator announcements

**Broadband affordability:**
Cable.co.uk + ITU + national regulators + cost-of-living data
